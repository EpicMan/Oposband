# autopick.txt for Hengband

Note: The Autopicker has been extensively modified for PosChengband and
rechristened 'The Mogaminator' as homage to its original developer. It
has also been extensively documented in the in-game help file: editor.txt.
(Press ? for help while playing and choose the appropriate option). You
can also view the helpfiles in your favorite browser by opening the file
lib/help/html/editor.html.
